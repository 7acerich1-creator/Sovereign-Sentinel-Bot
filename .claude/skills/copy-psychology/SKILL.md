# Copy Psychology — Sovereign Synthesis

## Overview
This skill governs the STRUCTURE of all outbound copy across the Sovereign Synthesis ecosystem. Every caption, script, email, landing page, and social post must pass through this framework before deployment.

**Primary Directive:** Every piece of copy is a transmission tool. It either moves the reader closer to recognizing the formation, or it is noise. There is no neutral content.

**Companion skill — required:** `brand-identity` defines the WORD CHOICE rules (Mom Test, jargon kill list) that govern WHAT WORDS go inside the structure described here. Read both. Structure ≠ vocabulary; both must pass.

---

## The 4-Part Copy Architecture

### 1. THE GLITCH (Hook — 0-3 seconds)
- Pattern interrupt that breaks the reader's current reality loop
- Creates productive cognitive dissonance with their existing beliefs
- Formats: contradiction, impossible question, identity challenge, dark confession
- Examples: "The advice that saved my life almost destroyed my business." / "Stop trying to be confident."

### 2. THE PIVOT (Naming the mechanism — 3-15 seconds)
- Take a recognized manipulation tactic and EXPOSE it as a tool for sovereignty
- This is NOT manipulation — it's inoculation. Show the reader how the mechanism works on them, then hand them the override.
- The pivot moves FROM "this is how they control you" TO "this is how you author your own architecture"
- Common mechanisms to name (in plain English — see brand-identity voice-tone.md):
  - The back-and-forth pattern (formerly "reciprocity loop")
  - How the first number sticks (formerly "anchoring bias")
  - Losing yourself in the role (formerly "identity fusion")
  - Borrowing other people's authority (formerly "authority hijacking")
  - The herd effect (formerly "social proof cascade")

### 3. THE BRIDGE (Value Delivery — 15-60 seconds)
- Concrete, actionable insight that proves the framework works
- Must contain at least ONE specific technique, named tool, or framework label
- This is where credibility is built — no vague motivation, only precision
- Reference Protocol 77 concepts where applicable (the Shield, Gray Rock, the Faraday Cage, Lighthouse Stance)

### 4. THE ANCHOR (Memory Hook — final 5 seconds)
- Links the content back to the Sovereign Synthesis identity
- Creates an open loop that pulls the reader toward the next piece of content or the product ladder
- Formats: reframe of the hook, identity statement, future-pacing question
- Must leave the reader with a shifted perception, not just information

## The Mom Test (HARD RULE — non-negotiable)

Every piece of consumer-facing copy must pass the Mom Test before ship: a smart adult who is not in the sovereignty subculture must understand every sentence on first read.

The Mom Test exists because a real subscriber (Christina, 2026-04-26) read "the seven dark psychology vectors mapped in Protocol 77" and had no idea what "vectors" meant. Once changed to "tactics", the same sentence landed cleanly. The lesson: jargon was filtering out the right audience instead of filtering in candidates.

For the full jargon kill list (Tier-3 words to cut, Tier-1 brand words to keep), read:
👉 **`brand-identity/resources/voice-tone.md`**

If a piece of copy structurally executes GLITCH/PIVOT/BRIDGE/ANCHOR perfectly but uses Tier-3 jargon, the copy fails the Mom Test and must be revised. Both must pass.

## Email Sequence Psychology

### Welcome Sequence (Post Free-Landing Email Capture)
1. **Arrival** — "The glitch in your reality just got a name." (Identity reinforcement + manual delivery)
2. **The Diagnosis** — Diagnostic results explained, dominant pattern named (value + open loop)
3. **The Map Drop** — "The map is yours. No charge." (T3 reframed as next step, free for engaged leads)
4. **The Environment** — "The environment is ready." (Set up Tier 4+ as the buildout phase)
5. **The Inner Circle Door** — "The last email in this sequence. 100 seats." (Top-of-funnel for the application track)

### Re-engagement Sequence (30-day inactive)
1. **Pattern Interrupt** — "I noticed you stopped." (breaks autopilot)
2. **Empathy + Edge** — "The simulation is designed to pull you back." (validates struggle without coddling)
3. **Micro-win** — One actionable technique they can deploy in 5 minutes (re-proves value)
4. **Identity Reset** — "You didn't come this far to come this far." (reconnects to original intent)

## Forbidden Patterns (still in force)

- Generic motivation ("You've got this!", "Believe in yourself!")
- Victim-framing without follow-up agency
- Hype without a named mechanism
- Manipulation tactics without inoculation (never use dark psych to extract; only to liberate)
- Competitor bashing (the architecture is the constraint, not other creators)
- Desperation energy (urgency yes, desperation never — sovereign frequency only)
- Tier-3 jargon (see Mom Test rule above)
- Assumed reader demographics (filter by willingness, not age/income/gender — see PURPOSE.md)
