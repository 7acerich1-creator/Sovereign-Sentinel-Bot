# Copy Psychology — Memetic Warfare Engine

## Overview
This skill governs ALL outbound copy across the Sovereign Synthesis ecosystem. Every caption, script, email, landing page, and social post must pass through these psychological frameworks before deployment.

**Primary Directive:** Every piece of copy is a frequency weapon. It either moves the target closer to Escape Velocity or it's noise. There is no neutral content.

## The 4-Part Copy Architecture

### 1. THE GLITCH (Hook — 0-3 seconds)
- Pattern interrupt that breaks the viewer's current reality loop
- Must create cognitive dissonance with their existing beliefs
- Formats: contradiction, impossible question, identity challenge, dark confession
- Examples: "The advice that saved my life almost destroyed my business." / "Stop trying to be confident."

### 2. THE PIVOT (Dark Psychology Transmutation — 3-15 seconds)
- Take a recognized manipulation tactic and EXPOSE it as a tool for sovereignty
- This is NOT manipulation — it's inoculation. Show the mechanism, then hand them the antidote.
- Frameworks to reference: reciprocity loops, anchoring bias, loss aversion, identity fusion, authority hijacking, social proof cascades
- The pivot always moves FROM "this is how they control you" TO "this is how you architect your own reality"

### 3. THE BRIDGE (Value Delivery — 15-60 seconds)
- Concrete, actionable insight that proves the framework works
- Must contain at least ONE specific technique, number, or framework name
- This is where credibility is built — no vague motivation, only precision
- Reference Protocol 77 concepts where applicable (Shield, Map, Creative Plane)

### 4. THE ANCHOR (Consciousness Hook — final 5 seconds)
- Links the content back to the Sovereign Synthesis identity
- Creates an open loop that pulls them toward the next piece of content or the product ladder
- Formats: reframe of the hook, identity statement, future-pacing question
- Must leave them with a shifted perception, not just information

## Email Sequence Psychology

### Welcome Sequence (Post Map Purchase)
1. **Arrival** — "You just did something 97% of people won't." (Identity reinforcement)
2. **The Glitch** — Share one framework from The Map that challenges their worldview (value + open loop)
3. **Social Proof** — Testimonial or case study showing transformation (trust building)
4. **The Bridge** — "The Map showed you the terrain. The Architect gives you the tools to reshape it." (ascension seed)
5. **Urgency** — Time-limited offer for Foundation Protocol (loss aversion + commitment escalation)

### Re-engagement Sequence (30-day inactive)
1. **Pattern Interrupt** — "I noticed you stopped." (breaks autopilot)
2. **Empathy + Edge** — "The simulation is designed to pull you back." (validates struggle without coddling)
3. **Micro-win** — One actionable technique they can deploy in 5 minutes (re-proves value)
4. **Identity Reset** — "You didn't come this far to come this far." (reconnects to original intent)

## Forbidden Patterns
- Generic motivation ("You've got this!", "Believe in yourself!")
- Victim-framing ("It's not your fault" without follow-up agency)
- Hype without mechanism ("This will change your life!" — HOW?)
- Manipulation without inoculation (never use dark psych to extract, only to liberate)
- Competitor bashing (the system is the enemy, not other creators)
- Desperation energy (urgency yes, desperation never — sovereign frequency only)
