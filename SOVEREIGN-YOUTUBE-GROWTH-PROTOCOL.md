## 1. STRATEGIC OVERVIEW

**Objective:** Build two YouTube channels simultaneously — one personal brand (Ace Richie / Sovereign Synthesis) and one anonymous top-of-funnel feeder (The Containment Field) — to generate audience, authority, and revenue toward the $1.2M liquid sum objective.

**Core Principle:** YouTube is the PRIMARY platform. TikTok and Instagram are DEFERRED. All energy, all resources, all agent bandwidth goes into YouTube until the engine is self-sustaining.

**Growth Model:** Search-first for the first 30 days, then pivot to Browse/Suggested as the algorithm learns the audience profile.

**Core Viewer Strategy (Three Tiers):**
- **Casual Viewers:** Browse-driven, watch 30-60 seconds, may not subscribe. Captured by thumbnails and hooks. They fund impressions but don't build the business.
- **Cold Viewers:** Search-driven, watch 3-5 minutes, evaluate whether to subscribe. The algorithm training phase (Videos 1-10) targets these — convert them to subscribers.
- **Core Viewers (THE TARGET):** Watch full videos, binge multiple sessions, engage with insights in comments, share with their network. These are the people who buy Protocol 77 and join Inner Circle. Every script, every visual decision, every CTA is engineered to build this tier. Reward people who watch to the end. Create binge-worthy series. Plant callbacks to previous videos. The core audience IS the business.

**2026 Algorithm Reality (Verified):**
- YouTube now tests new creators' videos with small audiences first. Strong engagement (watch time + CTR) triggers broader distribution. Even zero-subscriber channels can appear in recommendation feeds.
- **Satisfaction signals now outweigh raw watch time** as the primary ranking factor. YouTube evaluates not just whether people click, but what happens in the 30 seconds after they click ("Quality CTR").
- Shorts and long-form algorithms are **fully decoupled** — posting Shorts won't hurt or help long-form recommendations. Optimize each format independently.
- The Browse feed (YouTube homepage) uses deeper personalization based on watch history clusters. This is where most long-form discovery happens for established channels.

## 6. SEO & METADATA PROTOCOL

### Title Formula

- Structure: `[Emotional Trigger] + [Specific Topic] + [Implied Promise]`
- Keep under 60 characters when possible
- Front-load the keyword
- Never clickbait without delivering

### Description Protocol (Patch 5)

- **First 2 lines (first 200 chars):** Compelling summary with primary keyword in the first sentence. YouTube's algorithm prioritizes this for immediate search relevance.
- **Lines 3–5:** Chapter timestamps
- **Lines 6–8:** CTA (subscribe, email list, community)
- **Final section:** Natural language SEO summary as grammatically correct sentences with 1-2 LSI keywords. NEVER comma-separated keyword lists.
- **Target length:** 200–500 words for optimal SEO performance (verified 2026). YouTube crawls the full 5,000-character limit to build topical context.
- **Hashtag Limit:** 3–5 max. YouTube allows 15, but 3–5 provides strong categorization without clutter. Place in description (first 3 display above title).

### Tags

- **10–15 relevant tags** within 500-character limit (verified 2026: 5 highly relevant > 50 mediocre)
- Include: exact title, primary keyword, 2–3 related terms, channel name
- 5 highly relevant tags beats 50 generic ones

## 8. SHORTS STRATEGY

### Purpose

- Fastest subscriber acquisition lever for new channels (verified 2026: 74% of Shorts views come from non-subscribers)
- Feeds long-form algorithm by building subscriber base (verified: channels using Shorts + long-form grow 41% faster)
- Testing ground for hooks and topics
- **Fully decoupled from long-form algorithm** (verified 2026) — experiment freely without risk to long-form performance

### Execution

- **Start at Video 11** (after algorithm has initial long-form data)
- **Frequency: 2–3 Shorts per day** (verified 2026 optimal; space 4–6 hours apart so each gets its own algorithmic evaluation)
- **Length:** 30–45 seconds optimal
- **Content:** Repurpose highest-retention segments from long-form + standalone tests
- **Format:** Vertical 9:16, text overlay first 2 seconds, hard cut opening, cliffhanger ending
- **Hook:** First 1–3 seconds determine whether viewers swipe away — this is the make-or-break moment

### Shorts → Long-Form Bridge

- End every Short with: "Full breakdown on my channel"
- Pin comment linking to related long-form video
- Never duplicate full content between Short and long-form

### Shorts Monetization Path

- **YPP Tier 1 (early access):** 500 subscribers + 3 million Shorts views in 90 days → unlocks fan funding, channel memberships, Super Thanks
- **YPP Tier 2 (full ad revenue):** 1,000 subscribers + 10 million Shorts views in 90 days (or 4,000 watch hours long-form)
- Only **Engaged Views** count toward YPP eligibility (not autoplay impressions)

## 9. THUMBNAIL PROTOCOL

### The 3-Element Rule

Every thumbnail contains exactly 3 elements:
1. A face or figure showing strong emotion (or abstract symbolic equivalent for TCF)
2. Large readable text (3–5 words max, legible at mobile size)
3. A contrasting visual element (color block, arrow, symbol)

### Technical Specs

- 1280 x 720 minimum (16:9)
- High contrast — test at phone-screen size (70%+ traffic is mobile)
- Brand consistency: Ace Richie = dark + gold/amber, Containment Field = dark + green
- NEVER repeat the exact title on the thumbnail
- Generated via `generate_image` tool (Imagen 4, $0.02–0.06/image)

### Testing

- Create 2 variants per video
- Check CTR after 48 hours
- Swap if CTR below 4% (verified 2026: platform average is 4–5%)
